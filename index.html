How to Play

Objective: Survive to be the last remaining player

The goal of the game is to be the last player remainining. A player is out of the game when they lose all of their dice. Each round begins with a player placing a bid of how many of a chosen face they believe there are between all players. For example, if a player bids that there are two fours, they are claiming that between all players, there are at least two fours have been rolled. Once a bid has been made, any player can challenge the bid. Once a bid is challenged, all the dice are revealed and the either the bidder or the challenger lose a dice depending on the quantaty of the face bidded on. If no one challenges a bid, the turn proceeds to the next player who must either challenge the previous bid, or raise to a higher bid. The higher bid is only required to be higher in quantity, any face can be selected.
